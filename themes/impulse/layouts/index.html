Privacy Policy

Last updated: October 21, 2025

We take your privacy seriously. This page explains what data we (the creators of IMPulse — an open-source incident management system) do and do not collect when you visit our website.

1. What we collect

We use Google Analytics to understand how people use this site. This tool collects non-personal data, such as:

  • Pages visited
  • Time spent on the site
  • Browser type and operating system
  • Referring site or source
  • Approximate geographic region (not precise location)

This data is anonymous and used only in aggregate - we cannot and do not use it to identify individual visitors.

2. What we do not collect

We do not:

  • Collect names, email addresses, or any contact information
  • Use contact forms, comments, or account logins
  • Track users for advertising purposes
  • Sell or share any data with third parties

3. Why we collect analytics data

Analytics help us:

  • Understand what pages are most useful
  • See how visitors find our tool
  • Improve the website over time

4. Questions?

If you have any concerns about your privacy or this policy, feel free to contact us at support@impulse.bot.
